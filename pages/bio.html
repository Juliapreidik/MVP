My name is Mateo Varela. I was born in Canada and grew up in Rio Cuarto, Argentina. I'm currently living in Guelph, Ontario, Canada. I've been doing videography for a couple years. I started making videos while traveling as I was working for Air Canada and had the opportunity to explore different coountries. I have just recently decided to pursue it proffesionally.

Long term I would like to make videography/cinematography my career by doing music videos, weddings, dance videos, etc. I like to tell stories through videos which is what drew me to the courses I've done. I hope to gain more knowledge of my camera, how to tell stories through film, as well as lighting and sound techniques to make me a better cinematographer.
